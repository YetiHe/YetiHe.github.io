I'm a Master student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/), Baltimore, United States. I am co-advised by Dr. Ji Yi ([Yi Lab](https://www.yilab.org/)) in high throughput laser scanning microscopy, and Dr. Yu Sun ([Hopkins Computation Imaging Group](https://hcig.wse.jhu.edu/)) in generative models. Currently, I am focused on the integration of advanced computational models and imaging pipelines.

Before that, I was an undergraduate in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at [Southern University of Science and Technology](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/), Shenzhen, China, advised by Dr. Jianbo Tang ([Bio-Optic and Acoustic Technology Lab](https://sustech.edu.cn/en/faculties/tangjianbo.html)) in ultrasound imaging and optical coherence tomography. During this time, I was involved in ultrasound speckle decorrelation-based velocimetry and dynamic light scattering-OCT based quantitative imaging. 

Meanwhile, I am actively seeking 2026 Fall PhD positions.

My research interests include optical imaging, computational imaging, and ultrasound imaging.

I'm a Master student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/), Baltimore, United States. I am co-advised by Dr.Ji Yi in high throughput laser scanning microscopy, and Dr.Yu Sun in generative models. 

Before that, I was an undergraduate in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at [Southern University of Science and Technology](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/), Shenzhen, China, advised by Dr.Jianbo Tang in ultrasound speckle decorrelation and dynamic light scattering-optical coherence tomography.

Currently, I am focused on the integration of advanced computation algorithms and imaging pipelines. Meanwhile, I am actively seeking 2026 Fall PhD positions.

My research interests include optical imaging, computational imaging, and ultrasound imaging.
